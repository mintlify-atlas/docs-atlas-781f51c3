Color Flipper is a modern, interactive React 19 application that lets you generate random colors, switch between single-color and gradient modes, copy colors in HEX or RGB format, and maintain a persistent history of your favorite picks — all in a responsive, zero-dependency UI.

## What Color Flipper does

Color Flipper gives you a fast, visual workflow for discovering and capturing color values:

- **Generate** — click **Random** to instantly apply a new randomly-generated HEX color (or gradient pair).
- **Preview** — the full-bleed preview panel updates in real time as colors change.
- **Copy** — one click copies the formatted value to your clipboard with a brief toast confirmation.
- **Save & reuse** — colors are saved to a scrollable history panel and persisted across sessions via `localStorage`.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clone and install">
    Clone the repository and install dependencies with your preferred package manager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the dev server">
    Run `npm run dev` and open `http://localhost:5173` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your first color">
    Click **Random** on the preview panel to generate your first color, then click **Copy** to grab the value.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore gradient mode">
    Switch the **Mode** toggle to **Gradient** to generate two-color linear gradients.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Colors you generate are automatically added to the **History** panel and persisted in `localStorage`, so your palette is preserved between browser sessions.
</Tip>
